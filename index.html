THE LION

The lion (Panther leo) is a majestic big cat.

The second-largest after the tiger. It's known as the "king of the jungle" even though it primarily lives in savannas, grasslands, and woodlands in Africa and a small region of India.
Lions are social animals that live in groups called prides. Prides typically consist of related females, their cubs, and a few adult males. The females are the primary hunters in the pride, working together to take down large prey like zebras, wildebeests, and antelopes.
